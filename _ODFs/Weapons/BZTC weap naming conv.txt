Battlezone Total Command WEAPON PART NAMING CONVENTIONS
----------------------------------
FOLDER NAMING CONVENTION
Category+Combat Name+Opt Assault Name+Race (all seperated by 1 space)

Prefix Type:
C cannon    G gun    H hand    M mortar    N neutral    P pack    R rocket    S special/mine

Suffix Race:
A -American (NSDF)    B -Black Dogs    K -Chinese Red Army    R -Crimson Bears     S - Soviet Cosmo Communist Army     Y -Fury

ex - "R FAF Hornet a" - rocket/combatname/optional_assault_name/American_race
----------------------------------
POWERUP / WEAPON /ORDNANCE NAMING CONVENTION

Prefix Type:
ap - powerup
g - weapon
o - ordnance
sp - powerup spawn point

Suffix:
lowercase version of the above race letters

prefix/name/optionalcountnumber/suffix/attacktype
1-2 letters / 4 letters / 1 number / 1 letter / _a or _c
----------------------------------
4 EXAMPLES
Powerup:
apstbaa_c (ap  stba a _c)
ap - powerup
stba - weapon name (Stabber Anti-Tank)
a - race (American)
_c - combat version

Weapon:
gminis_a (g  mini  s _a)
g - gun (weapon)
mini - weapon name (Minigun)
s - race (Soviet/CCA)
_a - assault version

Ordnance:
osnipb_c (o  snip  b  _c)
o - ordnance (the bullet)
snip - ordnance name (Sniper Rifle)
b - race (Black Dogs)
_c - combat version

Spawn Powerup:
spflary (sp flar y)
sp - spawn powerup
flar - solar flare
y - race (Fury)
